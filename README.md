# Mixed Signals
A tool to help you design unique coctail recipes based on mood, taste palate, and alcoholic strength.

##Project Bakcground
Cocktails are incredibly delicious, but can get increasingly expensive.  I’m beginning to save money by experimenting with cocktail recipes at home, but a lot of the time I have no clue what I’m doing and what ingredients work well with what base drinks. On many occasions, I spend a long time googling recipes that look like they may taste bad to me. I wonder if there is a way to help me create cocktail recipes that fit my taste, and my taste only.
